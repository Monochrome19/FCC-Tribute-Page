More Facts About Blake:

  • As a Faunus, Blake possesses the ability night vision, allowing her to see proficiently in the dark.
  • Her weapon of choice is a Variant Ballistic Chain Scythe (VBCS) named Gambol Shroud.
  • She alludes to Belle, from the fairy tale Beauty and the Beast.
  • Her Semblance allows her to create Shadow-clones.
  • Blake is an ex-member of the White Fang.
  • Blake is not human, she is Faunus.
  • She's a member of team RWBY.
  • Arryn Zech is her voice actress.
  • Blake prefers tea to coffee.
  • 17 years old.
